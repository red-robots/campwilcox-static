Services

We serve individuals and businesses in all areas of investment planning, including:

Investments

We determine your overall investment needs and help you understand the importance of risk tolerance, diversification and time horizon. We’ll recommend the appropriate investment vehicles to help you achieve your financial goals. We build a comprehensive portfolio based on your unique needs.

Retirement Planning

We help you make the most of your employer-sponsored retirement plans and IRAs. We’ll determine how much you will need to retire comfortably. We help you plan for life events like college, marriage and retirement, for dreams and major purchases like a second home as well as future needs like health care. We serve our clients by helping them manage assets before and during retirement years.

Education Funding

We recommend investment and accumulation strategies to help you pay for your children’s education. To get ahead, your money has to grow faster than the cost of college — and when it comes to education funding, time really is money. We’ll help you select from multiple strategies like 529 plans, education savings accounts, and custodial accounts.

Access to Lending Services¹

Mangaing your liabilities are an integral part of your overall financial picture. Through Wells Fargo Advisors we offer access to lending services through dedicated affiliates.

Insurance and Risk Management²

We utilize in-house and otherexperts to review insurance policies for you to ensure the appropriate coverage is obtained. This service is essential to the planning needs of your financial future. Many people do not even think about reviewing an old policy to ensure it still makes sense to keep or update. At the Camp Wilcox Group, this is standard practice to help protect and preserve your wealth.

Annuities, Income Certainty and Legacy Planning

Based on in depth research and fact-finding, we work with you to determine what your overall income or legacy planning needs are and incorporate a portion of the portfolio to provide for these certainties. Based on what you communicate to us we will recommend solutions that make the most sense to accomplish your planning objectives.

Tax Management³

We help make sure your portfolio is tax-efficient. We develop tax-saving investment strategies that are tailored to your individual needs. Committed to a comprehensive approach, we work hand in hand with your qualified tax specialists to provide the most accurate guidance possible.

Trust and Estate Planning Services4

We work with our estate-planning and trust affilates and outside experts to review your wills and trusts to ensure we are preserving your estate for your intended heirs. Your needs and the needs of your family change over time, and as a result, your estate plan needs to be flexible and periodically reviewed. We will also assist with beneficiary designations, reduce potential exposure to estate taxes and probate costs, and coordinate with your tax and legal advisors.

  • Specialty Services (Real Estate, Closely Held and Estate Tax)
